Stuff I love & use

“Which product do you use to do x, y, z?” is one of the most frequently asked questions by my friends.
More and more new solutions come along as the Bitcoin development is maturing. There are so many fantastic Bitcoin-related products and services out there. I have invested tonnes of time testing new stuff and finding the products that align with my own values.
Here’s a list of things that I love and use. This list will grow and get updated over time with. The learning never stops, and I love learning!

Hardware wallet

BitBox02

My hodl wallet of choice.
Bitcoin-only.

Hardware wallet

Ledger Nano S

For frequent transactions.

Mobile wallet

Blue Wallet

Open-source. The awesome all-rounder on the go. Bitcoin, Lightning and watch-only.

Bitcoin node

MyNode

Open-source. What I use to run my own Bitcoin node.

Payment processor

BTCPay Server

Open-source. How I set up the Bitcoin payment processor.

Virtual hosting

Where I host my BTCPay Server.
