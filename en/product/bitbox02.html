Shiftcrypto BitBox02 Bitcoin-only edition

The latest Swiss-engineered hardware wallet



Why bitcoin-only edition

It is a hardware wallet with radically focused firmware, dedicated to supporting Bitcoin (BTC) exclusively. Less code means less attack surface, which further improves the security to give you peace of mind when hodling bitcoins.


Why Bitbox02

  • Offline backup and recovery with a microSD card
  • Native software client avoids security risks of browser-based clients.
  • Plausible deniability with hidden wallets and backups.
  • Portable case filled with epoxy and packed with security.
  • The subtle design avoids unwanted attention.
  • Fully open source.
  • On device display for secure transaction verification.
  • On device touch buttons for secure password and seed entry.
  • Swiss ideals: Quality and privacy without backdoors.
