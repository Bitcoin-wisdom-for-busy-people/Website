Bitcoin Wisdom for Busy People

ft. on Foster Inclusion podcast


I had the honour to be invited by Saidah Gomez-Fleury on her podcast, Foster Inclusion, to talk about my favourite subject, Bitcoin! We recorded this super fun conversation on 16 May 2020 and the episode was released a week later on 24 May 2021.

During our chat, we talked about how I fell down the rabbit hole, my view on Bitcoin, my approach in designing this website, where beginners can learn if interested, and some of the common red flags to look out for when you’re new to the world of Bitcoin.

Saidah and I met in early 2018 at the Crypto Valley Conference in Zug, Switzerland. At that time, I only moved to Switzerland from Australia about 6 months prior and was finding my way to get established in this new country and the blockchain industry. Since then, I’ve worked in the space, done my rounds in crypto valley, and decided to devote myself in Bitcoin education. Saidah took a break from the blockchain scene due to personal reasons but her interests in Bitcoin didn’t stop. She started a diversity and inclusion consultancy to help companies incorporate such culture.

We recently got reconnected and realised that our approach to Bitcoin is so similar. That sparks a lot of discussions and we decided to record the conversation. I definitely don’t fit into the normal stereotypical image of a Bitcoiner – male, geeky, white and so on. This will hopefully provide a different perspective and bring more diversity and inclusion in the Bitcoin space.

Saidah is an excellent interviewer and has a contagious positive energy. I hope you enjoy listening to the conversation and learn something from it!
